Our Story

Epiphanie and Théo have been building a life together, one shaped by curiosity, humor, and a love of travel. They’ve explored near and far, from U.S. cities to trips across Europe and Central America. France and the Philippines have been especially meaningful to them, and the Philippines will always hold a special place in their hearts as their favorite trip and where they got engaged. These days, their happiest moments are the everyday ones too, at home with their two cats.

Wedding Weekend

Before the wedding

Welcome to Poitiers

We will be staying in Poitiers as guests begin to arrive. If you plan to arrive in town ahead of the wedding weekend, please join us for a relaxed and informal start to the celebrations. We would love to spend time together before heading to the château.

Friday · September 11

Arrival at the Château Afternoon

Transportation from Poitiers to the venue will be arranged for those who need it. We will arrive at the château in the afternoon, giving everyone time to settle in and enjoy the surroundings.

Arrival Dinner 19:30

A casual arrival dinner will be hosted at the château. This evening is meant to be relaxed and convivial, a chance to gather, unwind, and begin the weekend together.

Saturday · September 12

Lunch at the Château 12:30

A light lunch will be served at the château for guests staying on site, providing a moment to relax before the afternoon celebrations begin.

Wedding Ceremony 15:30

Our wedding ceremony will take place in the afternoon, followed by time to celebrate together in the château grounds.

Vin d'honneur 17:00

Join us for drinks and small bites as we celebrate together after the ceremony.

Dîner de mariage 19:30

The wedding dinner will follow in the evening, with a long, joyful French-style dinner and celebration into the night.

Sunday · September 13

Farewell Breakfast 11:00

Let’s wrap up the weekend with breakfast. A final chance to mingle, share a meal, and say goodbye before your next journey.

Dress code

Friday Arrival Dinner 19:30

Smart casual
Please come dressed for a nice dinner, no sportswear, no shorts, no sneakers.

Saturday Ceremony, Cocktail Hour, and Dinner 15:30 onward

Formal
This is the main event. Suits or jackets for men, cocktail dresses or equivalent for women. Dress shoes expected. Please make the effort and dress up.

Sunday Farewell Brunch 11:00

Relaxed but put together
Keep it comfortable, but still presentable. No pajamas, no athletic wear.
